After you get in touch with AI models, you’ll agree that no two models are the same. Different models behave differently (GPT-5.2 Codex, Claude 4 Opus/Sonnet, Grok-3/4, DeepSeek-V3.2, Gemini 3, Llama-4 variants, Qwen3-Coder, etc.).

So, as a prompt engineer, the real expertise lies in selecting the right LLM for tasks and adapting and adjusting prompts with the model-specific nuances accordingly, instead of using one-size-fits-all, and also to strategically combine the base techniques to refine the output, cut hallucinations, fit context windows, handle complexity, save tokens, avoid redundancy, and ensure consistency without overload.

Phase 3 is more about experimentation and mastery. It is divided into two parts:  
1. Learning Model Sweet Spots and Weaknesses  
2. Advanced Prompting Strategies
