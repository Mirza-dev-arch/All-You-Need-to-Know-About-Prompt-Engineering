After you get in touch with AI models, you’ll agree that **no two models are the same**. Different models behave differently (GPT-5.2 Codex, Claude 4 Opus/Sonnet, Grok-3/4, DeepSeek-V3.2, Gemini 3, Llama-4 variants, Qwen3-Coder, etc.).

As a prompt engineer, the real expertise lies in **selecting the right LLM for the task** and adapting prompts with model-specific nuances — instead of using one-size-fits-all prompts. You’ll also learn to strategically combine base techniques to refine output, cut hallucinations, fit context windows, handle complexity, save tokens, avoid redundancy, and ensure consistency without overload.
